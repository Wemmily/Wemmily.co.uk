  • This website is an experiment, a challenge, a learning tool and a way for me to get myself online. The challenge was to learn to write code well enough that I could build something I was happy with, maintain it, and then push the boundaries of my knowledge in trying and testing new ideas using web as my 'meduim'.
  • I am currently a first year Design BA student at Goldsmiths, University of London.
  • I completed an Art and Design foundation at Central Saint Martins in 2016 where I specialised in Graphic Design // Illustration.
  • For Alevels I studied A2 Fine Art, Theatre Studies, and English Literature. I have an AS in Photography and Physics. My main output in Fine Art was mixed media- it was here I discovered a particular passion for printing.
  • These days my work tends to revolve around the briefs I'm set and the way I feel is best to respond to them- generally I select the medium based on the direction to take for the project, appling my creative skills as I feel appropriate. I enjoy this method of working as I feel I'm future proofing myself and at the same time making myself more flexible and versatile as a Designer.
