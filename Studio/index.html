This week in the Studio

Reflection w/b 27/11/17

This week we've been looking at the Matsutake Mushroom. At the beginning of the week I didn't feel like I knew anything about the Mushroom; I thought it was some kind of Magic Mushroom or Hallucinogen, which I have since learned is not true. The most interesting of the Mushroom's qualities for me was its rarity, and that is what the 'making' part project is built upon.
